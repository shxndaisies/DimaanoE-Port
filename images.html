Persona Articulata

Persona Articulata is a speaking pageant held during english month.
I was the chosen representative for my section and I won.

Booklandia

Booklandia is a character portrayal pageant also held during english month.
The representatives from each section portrayed characters from different literary works.

Book Parade

The book parade was part of the opening program for english month. Every
student got to participate in dressing up as their chosen book characters.

Science Element Pageant

The element pageant was a performance task for Science where students
portrayed different elements from the periodic table of elements in the style of pageantry.

Historical Icon

The Historical Icon competition is a pageant where a representative from each
section modeled a costume made of recycled materials that represented a historical figure.

Intramurals

The intramurals was a nice way to foster sportmanship among students.
